What is WhiteSpacer?

WhiteSpacer is a "slot machine" for uncovering new growth opportunities by randomly intersecting various aspects of your business. For example, you can randomly combine various customer segments with needstates and products on the menu to find opportunities for new offers. Or you could randomly combine products from the menu with each other and new ingredients to discover new possible bundling opportunities etc.

Usage:

  1. 1. tap WhiteSpacer icon at the top to randomize all options
  2. 2. slide coloured bands left or right to change a certain item
  3. 3. tap titles at the the top of each band to change what you are mixing
